## Text Risk Control

① "Subjective Experience" over "Objective Assertion"
? Logic: Non-medical products must never promise medical results.
❌ Violation: "Cured my acne" (medical outcome)
✅ Compliant: "My face feels much smoother, and the redness and swelling have gone down" (subjective sensation)
💡 Tip: Convert the "verdict" delivered from a god's-eye view into a "felt sense" from the user's perspective.

② "Scenario Description" instead of "Function Labels"
? Logic: Piling up functional keywords directly will almost certainly trigger system blocks.
❌ Violation: "Cures insomnia, powerful weight loss"
✅ Compliant: "Finally slept soundly, didn't wake up until sunrise; my waistband feels looser"
💡 Tip: Don't say what it can do — say what changed in your life after using it.

③ "Authentic Information" over "Bait-Style Marketing"
? Logic: Crackdowns are heavy on "selling dog meat as mutton" and on falsified materials.
❌ Violation: "Ice silk" (not a scientific name), "Click to claim cash" (no qualifying conditions stated)
✅ Compliant: "Polyester fiber" (national-standard name), "New registered users can claim a red packet" (clear conditions)
💡 Tip: Materials must be labeled with their scientific name; benefits must specify time limits and qualifying conditions.

④ "Reject Variants" — Reject "Burying Your Head in the Sand"
? Logic: Character variants are flagged as malicious marketing and lead to account de-prioritization.
❌ Violation: "g!ft", "ad-d me", "W-chat", "how much $$$"
✅ Compliant: "give to everyone", "service guarantee", "DM me", "price"
💡 Tip: Speak plainly and openly — don't try to outsmart the algorithm's detection.

## Core Violations Knowledge Base

"Absolutism & Exaggeration"
▪ Superlatives: best, Top 1, supreme, top-tier, ceiling of the industry, 100%
▪ Promise words: permanent, immediate results, rejuvenation in a day, one-time eradication, never rebounds
▪ Endorsement words: national-grade, special-supply, inspection-exempt, time-honored brand (without proof), RMB-banknote imagery

"Industry-Specific Minefields"
▪ Beauty & personal care: avoid — treat, detox, anti-inflammatory, fat-dissolving, whitening / dark-spot removal (without special certification)
▪ Food & health products: avoid — weight loss, virility enhancement, lowering "three highs" (blood pressure / sugar / lipids), brain-boosting, replacing medication
▪ Apparel & materials: avoid — "cashmere-feel" (≠ cashmere), "fur-and-leather integrated" (must meet the defined standard)

"Superstition & Social Climate"
▪ Strictly prohibited: "wife who brings husband good fortune", "wealth-attracting", "luck-changing", "secret recipes for having a son", PUA-style speech
▪ Strictly prohibited: glorifying getting-something-for-nothing, gender antagonism, flaunting wealth
