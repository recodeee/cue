# 1KCLUB — brand kit

Personal growth / engagement brand. Posts to @NagyVikt on X. Distinct
content lane from Volaria: this is the "help small accounts grow,
mutual-follow loops, reply games" content — designed to harvest
engagement and grow follower count, not project authority.

Keep 1kclub posts visually + tonally clearly separated from Volaria
(financial) so the @NagyVikt audience can tell them apart.

## Logo

`brands/1kclub/logo.png` — bold monogram / wordmark. Use it EXACTLY as
supplied. Never redraw, recolor, restyle.

## Palette

- **Pure white** `#FFFFFF` — primary background, type
- **Hot magenta** `#FF2D87` — primary accent (CTA, hook callout)
- **Electric yellow** `#FFE600` — secondary accent (energy, urgency)
- **Jet black** `#000000` — type on white, frames

High-contrast, social-native, scroll-stopper. Opposite of Volaria's
moody black-and-amber cinematic.

## Typography

- **Headline**: Anton / Bebas Neue / Druk Wide — heavy uppercase
  condensed, tight leading. Black on white preferred.
- **Body/CTA**: Inter / Manrope semibold, sentence case for warmth
- **One emoji max per tweet.** ✋ 🔥 ⚡ are house emojis — don't mix
  others in.

## Voice

Warm, direct, transactional. The unspoken contract is "you help me, I
help you." Talk like a person, not a brand. Lowercase OK, em-dashes OK,
contractions OK. Skip corporate hedging.

- ✅ "drop your color, we'll mutual-follow."
- ❌ "Engage with our community to grow together."

No "to the moon", no "let's gooo", no excessive emojis — those are
spam-coded and X downranks them. One hook + one ask per post.

## Platform

- **X is primary.** Threads/Bluesky optional cross-post for the same hook.
- **No LinkedIn.** Engagement-bait posts on LinkedIn nuke credibility.
- **No long threads** for this brand — single tweets that ask one thing.

## Card templates

### Template 1 — `engagement-reach` (default for follow loops)

Best for: reciprocal-follow posts, "comment X and I'll do Y", reply
games. Photo-led, text-light, designed to stop the scroll.

```
LAYOUT — single photo, no overlay text on the image.

Cinematic 4:5 portrait photo. Subject is a single hand or two hands
reaching outward toward the camera, palm forward, fingers slightly
spread. The hand is in sharp focus; the body and background fall off
into soft bokeh.

Lighting: warm, soft daylight from the side. Skin reads natural —
this is a real person, not a render. Athletic-wear or plain neutral
top is fine, no logos, no jewelry that fights for attention.

Background: neutral interior — slatted wood, soft white wall, or
out-of-focus gym — with one bright accent color (magenta or yellow
prop, or a single soft amber light source) so the frame doesn't go
flat.

Composition: hand in the lower-center third, eyes (if visible) in
the upper third. Leaves headroom for caption overlay if needed but
copy stays in the tweet body — image is clean.

Mood: inviting, "I'll pull you up" energy. Not aggressive, not
salesy. The kind of photo that reads as a gesture, not a stock pose.
```

### Template 2 — `bold-typo-card` (one-line statement)

Best for: hook posts that need to land without a photo. Pure type +
brand accent. 1:1 or 4:5.

```
LAYOUT — solid color background, oversized headline, brand mark
bottom-right.

Background: pure white #FFFFFF or pure magenta #FF2D87 (alternate
per post for variety).

Headline: heavy uppercase condensed sans (Druk Wide / Bebas Neue),
black on white OR white on magenta, 2-3 lines, perfectly kerned,
tight leading, left-aligned with breathing margin.

Bottom-right: small "1KCLUB" wordmark, same color family as headline,
20% the height of the headline. Subtle.

No emoji, no other graphics. Apple Keynote precision.
```

## Image-gen rules

- Use Higgsfield `nano_banana_pro` for typography-led cards (best text
  rendering) and `soul_2` for photo-led `engagement-reach` cards.
- Pass `brands/1kclub/logo.png` as a reference media ONLY for the
  `bold-typo-card` template (logo goes in-image). The
  `engagement-reach` template doesn't include the logo on the photo —
  the logo lives in the bio/profile, not the image.
- Always 4:5 vertical for X feed dominance.

## Don't

- Don't write threads for this brand
- Don't mix Volaria's color grade (teal-amber cinematic) into 1kclub
  images — fights the brand
- Don't add hashtags inside the image
- Don't post more than 2-3 follow-bait posts per week — X downranks
  pattern-spam, and the user's @NagyVikt audience overlaps with Volaria
- Don't run mutual-follow posts during Volaria publishing hours — split
  the cadence so the two voices don't collide on the same handle
